Cinnamon Cookies Recipe

Description:

Easy-to-make, Christmas' classic Cinnamon Cookies.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • Half cup of butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 and a half cups of flour
  • 1 and a half teaspoons of cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • (optional) Cinnamon sugar

Steps:

  1. In a mixer bowl, cream together sugar and butter; beat in egg and vanilla.
  2. Combine flour, cinnamon, baking powder and salt.
  3. Add to butter mixture and blend well.
  4. Cover and refrigerate 2 hours or till firm enough to roll into balls.
  5. Shape dough into small balls about 3/4-inch in diameter.
  6. Roll in cinnamon sugar to coat.
  7. Set cookies 1-inch apart on lightly greased cookie sheets.
  8. Bake at 350Â° for 10 minutes or till the edges are lightly browned.
  9. Cool slightly on pans, then remove to racks to cool completely.
